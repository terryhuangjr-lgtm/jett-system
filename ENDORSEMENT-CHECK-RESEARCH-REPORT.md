## Key Insights

The "Endorsement Check Problem" demonstrates:

1. **Purchasing Power Erosion:** Even billion-dollar deals lose value to inflation and opportunity cost
2. **Bitcoin Asymmetry:** A 10% allocation to Bitcoin would have produced life-changing returns
3. **Time Sensitivity:** Earlier deals (2014-2015) show exponentially higher returns
4. **Scarce Asset Appreciation:** Bitcoin's fixed supply vs unlimited dollar printing
