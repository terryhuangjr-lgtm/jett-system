# Maggie PM — AI Property Management System
**Prepared for:** Maggie Huang and Family
**Prepared by:** Terry Huang (Level Up Digital)
**Date:** May 2026

## Overview
A bespoke, AI-powered property management system designed specifically for the 18 properties you manage in NYC. Instead of clunky, off-the-shelf software, this system combines a clean overview dashboard with a 24/7 AI Assistant that you can talk to via text message. 

It tracks your tenants, leases, and tasks, and does the math on your rent collection automatically.

## Core Features (Phase 1)

### 1. The Command Center Dashboard
A custom-built, secure web portal to manage your entire portfolio at a glance.
* **Portfolio Overview:** Instantly see total properties, occupied units, total portfolio value, and monthly rent expected vs. collected.
* **Rent Status:** A color-coded tracker that shows exactly who has paid, who is pending, and who is late. Log payments with one click.
* **Lease Expiration Tracker:** Automatically flags any leases expiring within the next 90 days so you never miss a renewal window.
* **Task Management & Calendar:** Track maintenance, inspections, and renewals. The calendar automatically auto-generates rent due dates and lease expiration warnings without any manual entry.

### 2. "Hermes" — Your 24/7 AI Assistant
The dashboard is great, but the real power is the AI agent connected to it. You get a private Telegram bot that acts as your personal property management assistant. 

Instead of logging into a portal to check things on the go, you just text the AI in plain English:
* *"Who lives at 123 West 72nd and what is their phone number?"*
* *"What's on the schedule for this week?"*
* *"Who is late on rent this month?"*
* *"Thomas Park just paid his rent via Venmo."* (The AI will automatically log the payment and update the dashboard).
* *"Remind me to call the building manager for 200 West St tomorrow."*

### 3. Automated Morning Briefings
Every morning at 8:00 AM ET, the system automatically runs an audit of your entire database and texts you a concise morning report:
* Who is late on rent today.
* Any leases expiring soon that need attention.
* Urgent tasks due that day.
