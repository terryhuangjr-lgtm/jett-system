# Opportunities Scanner

## Purpose
Automatically scans for and tracks potential side hustles, business opportunities, and cash-flowing ventures.

## What Gets Saved Here
- Side hustle ideas matching your criteria
- Business opportunities worth evaluating
- Partnership/franchise leads
- Time-flexible income streams

## How It Works
- Daily background scan (low token usage)
- Findings saved to dated files
- Review when interested, ignore when not
- No alerts unless something exceptional

## Your Criteria
- Time-flexible (not owned by business)
- $2.5K+/mo potential
- Low/medium startup cost
- Leverage existing knowledge (sports, fitness, BTC)

---

Files will appear here as opportunities are found.
