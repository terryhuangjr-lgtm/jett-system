### Baseline: 5 points

### Contract Value (0-3 points)
- **+3 points:** $500M+ mega-deal (rare, massive attention)
- **+2 points:** $200M-$500M major deal (significant)
- **+2 points:** $100M-$200M notable deal
- **+1 point:** $50M-$100M decent deal

**Why:** Bigger numbers = more attention, clearer Bitcoin comparison

### Story Type (0-2 points)
- **+2 points:** Financial disasters (bankruptcy, fraud, lost fortune)
  - "Athlete loses $100M to bad advisor"
  - "NFL player files for bankruptcy"
  - "Fraud scheme costs player everything"
  - **Why:** Teaches POWERFUL lessons about custody and trust

- **+1 point:** Endorsements (Nike, Adidas, shoe deals)
  - "LeBron signs lifetime Nike deal"
  - **Why:** Alternative income streams interesting

- **+1 point:** Draft picks (rookie contracts, #1 picks)
  - "Top pick signs $50M rookie deal"
  - **Why:** Economics of draft fascinating

### Timeliness (0-2 points)
- **+2 points:** Breaking news (last 7 days)
- **+1 point:** Recent news (last 30 days)

**Why:** Fresh news gets more engagement

### Source Credibility (0-2 points)
- **+2 points:** Top-tier verified sources
  - ESPN, Spotrac, The Athletic, SI.com
  - Official league sites (NBA.com, NFL.com, MLB.com)
  - Yahoo Sports, Bleacher Report

- **+1 point:** Legitimate news sites (not Reddit/Twitter)

**Why:** Trust and accuracy matter

### Teaching Moment (0-2 points)
- **+1 point:** Contains financial education keywords
  - lesson, mistake, warning, advice, advisor
  - investment, financial, wealth, savings, retirement
  - tax, manage, planning

- **+1 point:** Clear Bitcoin angle (always present for money stories)

**Why:** Educational content is valuable content

### Viral Potential (0-1 point)
- **+1 point:** Superlatives or records
  - "Record-breaking contract"
  - "Highest paid athlete ever"
  - "Largest endorsement deal"
  - "First player to..."

**Why:** People share surprising facts

### Star Power (0-1 point)
- **+1 point:** Household names
  - LeBron, Curry, Mahomes, Messi, Ronaldo
  - Ohtani, Judge, Trout, Giannis, Jokic

**Why:** Big names = bigger engagement

### Maximum Score: 10 points
