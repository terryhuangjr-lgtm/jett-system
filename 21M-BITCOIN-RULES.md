# 21M Bitcoin Rules

## Content Guidelines

**For Bitcoin content:**
- Focus on: fixed supply, scarcity, halvings, fiat comparison, store of value
- Use verified facts from: Bitcoin whitepaper, Nakamoto Institute, CoinDesk, Wikipedia
- Connect to 21 Million theme: scarcity vs unlimited fiat

**Style:**
- Conversational, no emojis, no hashtags
- Short sentences for impact
- Connect Bitcoin properties to real-world examples

**Critical Rules:**
- Use verified facts only
- No approximations ("roughly", "about", "approximately")
- Include source when making claims
- BTC price at signing date for conversions

**21M Theme Examples:**
- "Only 21 million Bitcoin will ever exist"
- "Central banks print unlimited dollars"
- "Fixed supply beats printed money"
- "Scarcity is math, not policy"

**Sources:**
- Bitcoin Wiki: https://en.bitcoin.it/wiki/Genesis_block
- Nakamoto Institute: https://nakamotoinstitute.org/
- CoinDesk: https://www.coindesk.com/markets/halving/
- Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bitcoin
