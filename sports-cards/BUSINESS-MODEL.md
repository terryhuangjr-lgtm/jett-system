## Market Dynamics (Critical Understanding)

### **Values Are Constantly Changing:**
- Player performance (hot streak = prices spike)
- Trends (insert types go in/out of fashion)
- Influencers/big collectors (drive demand for specific cards)
- Season timing (load up before season if expecting callup/big year)
- Injuries (player hurt = hold until recovery)

### **Why Recent Comps Are Critical:**
- Market moves fast
- Yesterday's comp might not apply today
- Need real-time sold listings

### **Timing Is Everything:**
- Buy before hype builds
- Sell at peak performance
- Hold through injuries if long-term confident
- Know when to take profits
