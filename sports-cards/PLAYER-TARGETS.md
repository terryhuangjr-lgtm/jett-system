# Player Targets & Market Strategy

**Edge:** Look for underappreciated markets

## Market Strategy

**Buy:**
- Underappreciated 90s/2000s HOFers
- Under-the-radar rookies with potential
- Post-injury dips (talented players)
- Off-season (before hype builds)

**Avoid:**
- Peak hype rookies (Cooper Flagg level)
- "Read" in title (damaged cards)
- Overheated markets

**Hold:**
- Injured players (wait for recovery/return)
- Talented rookies having down years (Cam Ward)
