## Condition Evaluation

### Grading Criteria (In Order)
1. **Corners** - Sharp vs rounded/damaged
2. **Centering** - Image centered on card
3. **Surface** - Clean, no scratches/marks
4. **Edges** - Clean cuts, no chipping

### Red Flags
**"Read" in title** - AVOID ALWAYS
- Means "read the description" = damaged card
- Auto-skip these

### Evaluation Process
- Analyze eBay photos carefully
- Look for all 4 criteria
- Conservative = better (no surprises from PSA)

## PSA Details

**Frequency:** Monthly (target), sometimes miss months

**Turnaround Time:** 2-5 months (highly variable)

**Service Levels:**
- **Value tier** (default) - Keep costs low
- **Rush service** - For big cards with big profit potential
  - Example: 90s/2000s MJ inserts
  - Rush fee justified by profit margin

**Decision criteria for rush:**
- Expected profit covers rush fee with room to spare
- High-value card
- Want to capitalize on market timing
