# Booking Platform Research
**Date:** 2026-01-31
**Status:** Decision made - Mindbody selected

## Option 1: Use Established Booking Platform ✅ (BEST for you)

### Platforms that do this OUT OF THE BOX:

### 1. Mindbody (industry standard for fitness/sports) ⭐ **SELECTED**
- ✅ Handles membership tiers
- ✅ Booking caps and restrictions
- ✅ Automated billing
- ✅ Mobile app for members
- ✅ Integrates with Wix
- **Cost:** $129-169/month

### 2. Gymmaster or PushPress (for sports facilities)
- ✅ Built specifically for this
- ✅ Member management
- ✅ Booking restrictions
- ✅ Reporting/analytics
- **Cost:** $100-200/month

## Decision
**Terry's choice:** Mindbody

**Why Mindbody:**
- Industry standard
- Proven reliability for fitness/sports businesses
- Wix integration (website already on Wix)
- Full feature set out of the box
- Mobile app for members
